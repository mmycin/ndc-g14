Welcome to Notre Dame College, Dhaka

This is the official website of Group-14 of batch 2025


In the sacred enclave of Group-14, within the venerable halls of Notre Dame College, Dhaka, there resides a sanctum, a tapestry of intellectual pursuit woven into the very fabric of Room 317. This chamber, a luminous gem ensconced within the revered precincts of the Herington Bhaban, stands as a testament to erudition's lofty abode. Named in solemn tribute to the enduring legacy of Father John J. Harrington CSC, whose sagacious stewardship graced the institution from 1949 to 1954, it echoes with the footfalls of scholarly pilgrimage.
This edifice, a bastion of learning, dons the regal cloak of august nomenclature, a homage to a luminary whose wisdom still whispers through the corridors. Within these academic ramparts, 131 minds, each a seeker of knowledge, converge in a symphony of intellect, each note resonating with the echoes of scholastic pursuit and communal enlightenment. The scholastic cohort, draped in the mantle of Group-14, becomes a living poem, navigating the corridors of wisdom with an indefatigable spirit, etching a legacy in the annals of Notre Dame College that reverberates through time like verses in a celestial manuscript.
